About BB Apron

At BB Apron, we believe that aprons are more than just kitchen wear – they’re a reflection of personality, style, and purpose. Whether you're a professional chef, a passionate home cook, or someone who loves to craft, our aprons are crafted to match your lifestyle.

Founded in the heart of London, BB Apron brings together tradition and modernity by offering a wide range of aprons – from classic designs to bold, modern styles. Our mission is simple: to provide high-quality, comfortable, and fashionable aprons that empower our customers.

We’re proud to support local artisans, sustainable materials, and ethical production. Every BB Apron is designed with care, built to last, and made for those who lead with creativity.
